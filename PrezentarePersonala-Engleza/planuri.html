Future Plans

What will I do in the future?

    A very hard question. If you asked me that a few years ago, maybe before I go to the high school, or even earlier, I would have known what I wanted to do next: a high school student who always tries to be the best, something he still works on.

    Now that question is far more difficult, she puts me more on thoughts than before. Going to high school, I realized that if I want to get someone I need to work first and that anything you propose is not impossible. I came to the Mathematics-Informatics profile from the desire to learn a lot and to focus on what I like: informatics and exact sciences. For a few years I've been thinking about going to college abroad, in England for example, after which I will return in the country. However, as the days pass, my plans are changing, not knowing what faculty or job I would like to follow. Mostly, I want to continue with informatics or something close to it. I would like, as in the future, to be an astronaut or a programmer engineer, or even a man recognized worldwide for his work, but I do not know what chances I would have, taking into account that it will be very difficult and will intervene, along the way, many other temptations.

    Personally, I hope to succeed in everything I want. I want to set up a beautiful family and to keep my close friends all my life. I will try to have a well-paid job and to work where I like.

    In the end, I hope I can successfully lead all my wishes, no matter how complicated they are, and for the rest to enjoy any moment of my life.

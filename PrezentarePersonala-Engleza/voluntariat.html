Volunteer

What do I Do in my free time?

    One of the things that I liked to do whenever I have the occasion is to be volunteer. I have akways liked to offer my help for almost everything without receiving anything in return and, so, I can bring a change. I do that thing with the thought that this will really help me in life, gaining experience and having unique adventures. I think that everyone in their life should try at least once to volunteer, in whatever they want, and surely they will not regret the decision.

UN Youth Association of Romania - Roman Club

    I am a part of this organization since March 2017 and it is by far one of the most beautiful experiences which I had. I signed up at the suggestion of some of my friends and I opted for a place in Public Relations Department, where I am even today. At the beggining I thought my place was not there and the concept on which it is based is not for me, but with time I realised that I was wrong and I would have done a stupid thing if I had given up. Since I am in association I learned a lot of things which will help me much from now on. I became much more sociable and now I can easily talk in front of the others, even if some difficulties aries. Thanks to the organization I now know how to coordinate a project and how write a draft and to make posters and certificates for different projects or events. Some of the projects I participated in or to whom I helped since I am in the association are: Finance Balance 2017, Roman MUN 2018, YouthFEST 2017 and 2018, Karaoke Night 2017, BlecherFest 2017 and 2018, Learn About 2017 and many, many others.
